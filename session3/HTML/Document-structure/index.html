Paragraphs are often made up of several sentences. There are usually between three and eight sentences. In most organized forms of writing, such as essays, paragraphs contain a topic sentence .

Subsection

Paragraphs can begin with an indentation, or by missing a line out, and then starting again.

This makes it easier to see when one paragraph ends and another begins

Each paragraph may have a number of sentences, depending on the topic.
